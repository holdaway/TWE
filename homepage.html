Dependable. Professional. Affordable

Trans-Western Electric Ltd. is a Province of British Columbia based, unionized company, located in the Municipality of Langley.

Since the Company was incorporated 36 years ago we have specialized in the areas of street lighting, traffic signalization, area lighting, parking lot lighting and all types of recreational field or court lighting. We have performed this work for commercial, industrial, municipal and private projects in various locations throughout the lower mainland, interior and Vancouver Island. Our quoted prices are competitive and our staff is very professional and competent.

Safety

TWE Ownership and Management is committed to providing a safe and healthful workplace with a comprehensive and compliant Health and Safety Program for all employees. As part of our continued commitment to safety TWE became COR certified in 2018.

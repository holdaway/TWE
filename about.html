About Us

Trans-Western Electric Ltd. (TWE) is a family owned and operated electrical company. We are located in beautiful British Columbia with our main office in Langley and a smaller office located in West Kelowna. Since our incorporation in 1980 we have specialized in the supply and installation of streetlights, traffic signals, high mast lighting, area lighting, parking lot lighting, various types of sports field lighting and sign bridges. We have performed this work for commercial, industrial, municipal and private projects in various locations throughout British Columbia including in the lower mainland, interior and Vancouver Island.

While the majority of our work is procured independently a number of our larger projects are the result of successful design build team projects. Our electricians are proud members of IBEW Local 213 and our civil employees proud members of CSWU Local 1611.
